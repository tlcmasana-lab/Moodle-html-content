Dual grading system
Every Workshop student gets TWO grades: Submission grade (quality of their work) + Assessment grade (accuracy of their reviews). Both go to the gradebook.

How assessment grade is computed
  • 1
    Teacher records reference scores per criterion for each submission
  • 2
    Each peer's scores are compared criterion-by-criterion against the reference
  • 3
    Deviation calculated — smaller deviation = higher assessment grade
  • 4
    Teacher can override any grade manually for exceptional cases
